3. The 21st Ifra Business Expo 2023

Date : 25 - 27 August 2023

Asosiasi Franchise Indonesia (AFI) & Dyandra Promosindo mempresentasikan International Franchise, License and Business Concept Expo & Conference (IFRA) ke-21 tahun 2023.
